Brief Description of Business Operation:

The Brew and Coffee Shop aims to earn profit and promote high-quality coffee. The business will cater to the needs of its target demographics by providing quality products and sold-out. The business will offer convenience and efficiency in the services that will be provided to the customers. The business will make use of social media platforms to establish its presence within the industry and introduce itself to the public as an environmentally sustainable business. Furthermore, the business will communicate directly with the local producers and crop breeders of the products to ensure the quality of the products that will be offered in the coffee shop.
